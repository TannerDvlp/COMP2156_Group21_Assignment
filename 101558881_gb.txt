George Brown is a college known for their practical and hands-on learning. There are three campuses students can choose to attend. St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront. Each Campus specializes in certain fields. St, James is known for business, hospitality, culinary arts and more. Waterfront is known for their focus on health sciences. And lastly, Casa Loma is known for technology, construction, and engineering programs. Students should choose george brown to have hands-on experience working in their field and learn from the professors who have real world experiences in the field you choose to pursue and learn from them.
